Zoë Bell (she/her)

TA • zbell@ • website

I am a 2nd-year CS theory PhD student here at Berkeley. I did my undergrad at Harvey Mudd College in Southern California, where I majored in mathematics and did a minor in STS (Science, Technology, and Society). (One day I hope to complete my journey back up the coast to my hometown of Seattle…) Not only do I love doing the math puzzles which are the heart of algorithms, but I am also fascinated by how proofs produce certainty, the deeply social and collaborative nature of doing mathematics, and what more might be asked of us beyond traditional correctness and efficiency when we contextualize these algorithms in their social contexts. If any of these topics sound interesting to you, I am always excited to discuss them!
